2019 San Francisco Shock Playoffs Heroes Usage

Heroes Usage

Each team have professional players that play certain roles and preferences of heroes that encapsulates their play style and comfortability. More accordingly, players choose heroes based on the current trending meta. Whichever hero they choose, each hero can compliment together for a synergetic team composition. Throughout the meta, there have been developed certain synergies' of heroes and some work better than others or some style of play is preferred over another, it just depends on what tactics each team would choose to deliver. Below are each San Francisco Shock player's heroes usage during the 2019 Playoffs. Each usage of their chosen hero tells a story. More information about each Hero found here.

San Francisco Shock's Hero Usage

Each percentage is calculated based on the players' specific hero play time divided by the total play time of all preferred heroes. For instance, Architect has an ~80% usage on Bastion out of the total play time percentage of his chosen heroes. Each color of the bars are based on each Heroes' thematic colors within the game. This decision kept the aesthetic of the graph to adhere to each heroes' respective theme. Depending on the players' role, there are certain heroes that fit the player's role criteria, so not all players can play another heroes' role. See General Game Info for more information on player roles.

Based on the graph above, the San Francisco Shock's Hero Usage displays the utilization of a double shield oriented team in dueling their opponents. Pointing out Tank players, with Choihyobin on Sigma and smurf on Orisa—both heroes that provide shields—this duo of heroes easily signifies their shield oriented team compositions and selections of the trending meta. With accompanying Support roles such as moth on Lucio and Viol2t on Moira, supports the team that gives speeding in to attack or to escape in varying situations along with high healing capacity from Moira's kit of abilities.
