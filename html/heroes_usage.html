Below are each San Francisco Shock player's heroes usage during the 2019 Playoffs.

Each player plays a specific role and within their specific roles, they play heroes that encapsulates their play style and comfortability. Based on the chart above, the San Francisco Shock hero usages displays a very brawl style team in dueling their opponents. Noting tank roles, super on Reinhardt and sinatraa on Zarya, this duo signifies their brawl style compositions. While accompanying support roles such as moth on Lucio and Viol2t on Moira, part of a team that has speed to attack or escape when in varying situations and a high healing capacity.
