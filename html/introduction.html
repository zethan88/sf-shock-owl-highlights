Description

San Francisco Shock is a professional American esports team representing San Francisco, California, in the Overwatch League. The Shock compete in the Western Division. Founded in 2017, the SF Shock is one of twelve founding members of the Overwatch League and one of three Overwatch teams in CA. The franchise is owned by Andy Miller, co-owner of NRG Esports and the NBA team Sacramento Kings.
