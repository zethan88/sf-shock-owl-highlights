2019 Overwatch Leauge Awards For Performances

The Overwatch League gives out three awards for the playoffs, the Dennis Hawelka, Rookie of the Year, and Most Valuable Player. The Dennis Hawelka is given to the player that had the most positive impact in the community, named after the late Dennis Hawelka. The Rookie of the Year is given to a league player with the strongest and most impactful performance in their first year. The Most Valuable Player award is given to the player with the most impactful performance throughout the entire season. Overall, in the Overwatch League, the Dennis Hawelka award was awarded to Scott “Custa” Kennedy of the Los Angeles Valiant, the Rookie of the Year award was awarded to Hyo-Jong “Haksal” Kim of the Vancouver Titans, and finally the Most Valuable Player was awarded to Jay “sinatraa” Won of the San Francisco Shock.
