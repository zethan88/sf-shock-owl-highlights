2019 San Francisco Shock Player Performances

Player Performances

Below are each visualization of the San Francisco Shock player's performance during the 2019 Playoffs.

This bar chart displays the players' performance based on the sum of damage done and healing done. Right of the bat, two players with high damage done and healing done are Choihyobin and Viol2t, respectively. It can be clearly deduced that the tank duo Choihyobin and smurf display a massive amount of damage output as front-liners. And the coming second pair, STRIKER and Viol2t, display their output of damage as DPS and Flex roles, respectively. Onto healing done, the clear main support duo are moth and Viol2t, with Viol2t leading the amount of healing output. This graph also signifies Viol2t's excellent performance: second in damage done and top in healing done.
