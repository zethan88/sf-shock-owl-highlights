2019 Playoff Team Performance Comparisons

Team Performances

Here displays the San Francisco Shock's performance compared to another team. In this case we are comparing the San Francisco Shock against the Vancouver Titans during the 2019 Grand Finals.

This comparison displays the teams' time stayed on point in fighting for the objective. Comparing the San Francisco Shock Objective time to the Vancouver Titans Objective time, the San Francisco Shock have the higher time on all map types except Assault.

Team Comparison Based On All Damage Done Per Eliminations

This comparison displays the teams' all damage done per elimination. The San Francisco Shock has one of the lowest eliminations as well has the highest eliminations. Compared to the Vancouver Titans, their stats remain in between the SF Shock. However, the San Francisco Shock also have the highest amount of damage done with over 50,000 points.
