2019 San Francisco Shock Regular & Playoff Season Record In a Timeline

Above displays the 2019 San Francisco Shock's Regular & Playoff Season Timeline. The San Francisco Shock represented in their signature orange themed color and the enemy teams represented in gray as their competing teams. The timeline is interactive to hover each bar that reveals more information about the date of the match, their opponent, and the outcome of the match in favor of the San Francisco Shock point of view. As seen, the San Francisco Shock blew the competition throughout the season. Due to their strong starting roster lineup and the GOATS meta, the San Francisco Shock became the leading team in dominating this meta. With a majority of their games played ending in a 4 - 0 record. Some matches were close, but in the end, the San Francisco Shock came out on top. Entering the playoffs and grand finals with role lock introduced, the San Francisco Shock still exuded excellent performances and domination over the competition.
